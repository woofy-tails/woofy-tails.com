About Us

‘Woofy Tails’ is a single platform which provides all types of pet services for pet parents including boarding, house sitting, day care, walking, grooming, accessories, food, travel, show & events, breeding, medical care services, training and adopting a pet.

Vision

‘Woofy Tails’ is an initiative that focuses on revolutionizing the pet care industry by providing all necessary pet essential services on one platform. We want to bring a change in the current pet care industry by fixing some of the key inefficiencies faced by pet owner.

Mission

‘Woofy Tails’ wants to reduce the need for searching different websites and apps for diverse needs of our kiddo and want to connect different stakeholders of pet industry like veterinarians, breeders, pet food suppliers, pet groomers, pet trainers, pet sitters etc. at one marketplace becoming the one stop solution for every pet parent.
